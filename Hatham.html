Special Operation: Gorrethian Exfiltration

Overview

Rendezvous: Starfleet Starbase 432, Wayfarer Expanse

Destination: To be learned on site

While it is expected that you will be traveling to Ralos, that information may not be known in advance by the identity you are likely to assume. The less you know in advance, the more convincing your questions will be.

Faction of Note: High Gorrethian Kingdom

The High Gorrethian Kingdom is an interstellar species about which the Romulan Republic knows little. It is in the interest of your assumed identity to learn more about them to report back to the senator in order to better understand Starfleet's interests in rumored negotiations with them.

Objectives

Primary Objectives

  • Identify and assist the Qowat Milat Nehvan in the rescue of Takanet's Beloved.
  • Identify and assist Takanet's Beloved.
  • Obtain as much information on the Gorrethian Kingdom as possible.

Secondary Objectives

  • Establish rapport with Takanet and her friends
  • Assess the readiness of the team assembled for the rescue, and include their composition and histories in your report.
  • If a situation arises which should result in the need for sacrifice, make it look like the Tal Shiar sent you then and only then.

Contact

Name: Nehvan

Position: Sister of the Qowat Milat

Little is known about this particular sister of the Qowat Milat which would have been likely provided to the identity you will assume. Consequently, it would be in your best interest to learn what you can about them. Know the common traits of the Qowat Milat include honesty, directness, surety of purpose, and a willingness to cut down anyone or anything that stands between them and the one to whom they have sworn their blade. In this case, it is anticipated but "unconfirmed" that this Qowat Milat will be taking action on behalf of either the Gorrethian Takanet Armun'he.

Assumed Identity

Full name: Hatham tr'Dharvanek

Position: T'virikhan Lieutenant, Shuttle Pilot

A native of Valcaria and former member of T'virikhan's crew, Hatham has reenlisted to serve on the warbird again. He is a pilot for the House and is on the roster for shuttle pilots that occasionally serve the senator's office.

This "shuttle pilot" is responsible for bringing you and your partner to Starbase 432 in the Wayfarer Expanse. Your skills both as a pilot and history of military service has earned, by promotion or demotion, whichever seems most consistent with the responses of your contacts, the rank of sublieutenant.

Your newfound loyalty to the Romulan Republic and its ideals of independence and self-actualization are reflected in this mission for which you are a ready volunteer. Having heard from an aide to the senator the plight of the Gorrethian, you were ordered in secret to personally see to the well-being of the Qowat Milat, to serve as a back up pilot for her crew, and to provide protection for Takanet's beloved whom we need returned alive in order to ensure that the Gorrethian Kingdom's leverage is in more trustworthy hands.

Associates

Full name: Ejiul tr'Mendak

Position: Senatorial Aide

An Unsa'avit who could easily pass for Vulcan, Ejiul has been in Ailak's employ long enough that he is now an elder. While not officially on Ailak's security team, Ejiul's activities say another story. Some records indicate that the Senator may consider them a disposable agent, but their long history of service suggests that their skills have not degraded.

This is the individual your identity is assigned to transport and support in the work with Nehvan.

Full name: Ailak ir-Valcaria e-nRehfirh tr'Stelravas

Commonly used name: Deihu Ailak tr'Stelravas

Position: Senator

House s'Stelravas were a wealthy but not politically important house before the supernova, notable for patroning underprivileged artists and scientists. Ailak married in from Unsa'avit clan s'nRehfirh, a scandal at the time, to strengthen the House with Noble Born blood. He was known mostly as Commander of the exploratory warbird T'virikhan, which saw battle during the turbulent 2370s.

Less-secret-now, many of the House's retainers had Unificationist or other dissident ties. When rumor of the supernova first hit the Senate, he used T'virikhan to evacuate as many of his people as possible against orders. As a result, the House survived the supernova with clan and client bloc intact, went rogue (confirming rumors of dissident ties), and became politically significant in one swoop. He currently represents their new settlement on Mol'Rihan, but has handed duties off to a young relative of his wife who is expected to be his successor to the Senate.

It is a badly-kept secret that he plans to retire to command a ship again, and he has poured most of his time, energy, and finances into an expensive refit. "Ship-Clan T'virikhan" (as it is jokingly called but not to Ailak's face) is not likely to remain on Mol'Rihan for long. Those who know Ailak personally are struck by his singleminded devotion to the stars and romanticism about the Sundering and Exodus, which some consider to be misplaced.

A common thread for this senator is that he employs family and those who served under him on the T'virikhan. If there is ambiguity about how you came to know the senator, service is the place to start.
