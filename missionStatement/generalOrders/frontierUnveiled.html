Frontier Unveiled

Order Parameters

Exploration and Discovery:

  1. The primary mandate of the 47th Fleet is to explore and document all celestial phenomena, planetary bodies, and civilizations encountered within the designated region.
  2. Priority shall be given to the investigation of anomalies, wormholes, and any spatial or temporal disturbances that may be encountered.
  3. Research and investigation are paramount. Fleet vessels shall be equipped with updated sensors to maximize data collection and analysis.

Diplomacy and Peaceful Engagement:

  1. First Contact protocol is in effect at all times when engaging new civilizations. The Fleet shall strive to establish peaceful relations with any newly encountered species or civilizations.
  2. Diplomatic personnel shall be present on all operational or engaged vessels to facilitate peaceful interactions and cultural exchange. If a vessel or outpost lacks this, an officer(s) will be sent to SB432 for properly diplomatic training courses.
  3. Any potential threat shall be dealt with through diplomacy and non-violent means whenever possible. This can be interpreted when needed by Commanding Officers of Officers of the Watch pending hostile encounters.

Resource Management and Sustainability:

  1. All Fleet operations shall prioritize the conservation and preservation of natural resources, ecosystems, and cultural heritage. Even for unknown and non-Federation worlds.

Non-Interference Directive:

  1. The Prime Directive (General Order 1) shall be strictly upheld. Fleet personnel shall refrain from interfering in the natural development of civilizations, especially pre-warp societies.
  2. Any violations of the Prime Directive shall be reported immediately, and corrective actions taken as necessary. If an officer fails to report misconduct with the Prime Directive, they can be held responsible as well.

Defensive Measures:

  1. While the primary goal is peaceful exploration, the 47th Fleet is authorized to defend itself and protect the Federation's interests and SB432 when faced with hostile actions.
  2. All vessels shall maintain combat readiness, and defensive measures shall be proportional to the threat encountered, as per the standard of current-times.

Regular Reporting:

  1. Comprehensive mission logs, including scientific findings, diplomatic interactions, After Action Reports and notable events, shall be submitted to Command on a regular basis.
  2. The Fleet Command Admiral shall provide monthly status reports to keep Starfleet Command informed of mission progress.

Adherence to Federation Values:

  1. All personnel are reminded of the importance of upholding Federation values, including tolerance, diversity, and the pursuit of knowledge for the betterment of all sentient beings.
  2. Respect for local customs and traditions shall be observed at all times.

Effective Notice

This General Order is effective immediately and shall remain in effect until modified or rescinded by Starfleet Command. The 47th Fleet is entrusted with the responsibility of expanding the boundaries of knowledge and the Federation in the newly dubbed "Wayfarer Expanse" of the Alpha Quadrant out of the Kreel Sector.
