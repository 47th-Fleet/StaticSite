Standing Orders

Context

Newly raised by station on the edge of the Alpha Quadrant, Starbase 432 lies nearest to the uninhabited Kreel System who is nearest to the Cardassian Union, Ferengi Alliance and a small pocket of Federation territory. With the Klingon Empire, Federation and Romulan Republic finally on on friendly terms, Starfleet has chosen to Boldly Go once more. This includes exploration of this unclaimed and lightly mapped region. But most of all, to stand as symbol of hope and unity where the Federation has little representation.

Orders

The standing orders set by Starfleet Command are as follows:
  • To keep peace in the sector.
  • Engage in the Prime Directive when engaging with new cultures and life forms in the area by making First Contact.
  • Manage and host any Klingon or Romulan vessels needing repairs or respite.
  • To explore the surrounding systems and spatial anomalies that have recently been detected in the area.
  • Maintain Federation law and customs in-and-around Starbase 432. Although the reach of the station is limited.
  • To keep cautious of any conflicting interests with the neighboring Cardassian Union or Ferengi Alliance. Statfleet wants
  • no feathers ruffled if it can be helped.
  • Prepare the Starbase for upcoming fleet operations (future ship RP Hooks/future Captain alts!)

Reassignment Statement

Now, assigned by Starfleet Command, the crew of 432 is being delivered to the starbase to report for duty, having been reassigned from previous posts or recently graduations from Starfleet Academy.
